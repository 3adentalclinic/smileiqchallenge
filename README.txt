THE SMILE IQ CHALLENGE  ·  3A Dental / BNI Jeet
================================================

WHAT IT IS
A one-page game to send your chapter before your feature. Two scored rounds
(a myth-or-true quiz, then "spot the referral" using your real trigger lines),
a "beat my score" share loop, your one-sentence referral script, and your links.

No date, time, or venue is mentioned anywhere. No invented patient story.
Everything matches your actual Canva feature deck.

PUT IT ON GITHUB (2 minutes)
1. Create a new public repo, e.g.  smile-iq
2. Upload BOTH, keeping the structure:
       index.html
       images/   (the whole folder)
3. Repo > Settings > Pages > Branch: main, folder: /(root) > Save
4. Share the link it gives you:
       https://<your-username>.github.io/smile-iq/
   The share buttons inside use that link automatically.
