THE SMILE IQ CHALLENGE  ·  3A Dental Clinic
============================================
A universal, shareable lead magnet. One file. Fully self-contained.

ONE FILE, NOTHING ELSE
Every image is baked inside the HTML, so it always shows up, even opened
straight from your phone or attached anywhere. No images folder needed.

PUT IT ONLINE (so the share buttons make a clean link)
1. New public GitHub repo, e.g.  smile-iq
2. Upload the one file. Rename it to  index.html  (so the URL is clean).
3. Settings > Pages > Branch: main, /(root) > Save.
4. Share the link it gives you:  https://<username>.github.io/smile-iq/
   Put it in your Instagram bio, WhatsApp, website button, anywhere.

WHAT'S INSIDE
- Round 1: a 6-question myth-or-true quiz (surprising, plain language).
- Round 2: "Who needs a dentist?" 6 everyday lines, 4 need care, 2 do not.
- A score out of 12 with a shareable "challenge a friend" loop.
- A soft, honest "what to do next" with a WhatsApp button.
- Trust block (40 years, one roof, the three of you + real photos).
- Save-our-details: TapOnn card (QR + tap), WhatsApp, call, website,
  Instagram, Google Maps.

NOTES
- No mention of any chapter, presentation, date, or venue. Works for anyone.
- Closes on "Crafting Smiles, Transforming Lives" (your all-channels tagline).
  If you want it removed or changed, just ask.
- To swap a photo later, send me the new image and I will rebuild the file.
